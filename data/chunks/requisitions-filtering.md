## Context

The Requisitions tab provides filtering and sorting capabilities for different requisition details. This includes information about facilities, coordinators, job titles, requisitions, shifts, status columns, and more.

## Response

To use the filtering features on the Requisition tab:

1. Navigate to the Requisition tab.
2. Use the filtering features to target the desired requisition details. Filters include columns such as Facility, Coordinator, Specialty/Dept., Job Title, Requisition Name, Shifts, Days Posted, Start/End Dates, and Candidate Status.
3. To activate the filters, press the 'Go' button. To reset the filters and remove applied settings, press the 'Rest' button.
4. Hover over the blue pinpoint icon next to the facility to view the exact location details.
5. Click on any blue-text requisition name link to open a full pop-up requisition details view.
