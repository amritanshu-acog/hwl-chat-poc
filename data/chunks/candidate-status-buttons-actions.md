## Context

Under the Candidate Status column on the Requisition tab, various icons indicate the current status or action available for proposed candidates undergoing onboarding.

## Response

The following actions are available under the Candidate Status column:

1. **Sign Agreement**: Indicates the facility has initiated an agreement pending signature from the agency.
2. **View Agreement**: Allows agencies to view or modify the agreement prior to finalization.
3. **View Attestations**: View or modify the attestation questions answered at the time of the proposal.
4. **View Schedule**: Enables agencies to view the full schedule for a candidate.
5. **Incomplete Profile Sections**: Shows which credentials are unsatisfied when hovering over the option.
6. **Download Signed Agreement**: Access and download the fully signed agreement.
7. **Credentialing Error Review**: HWL Compliance has flagged an issue with the candidateâ€™s credentialing documents, requiring action.
8. **Withdraw Candidate**: Use this option to withdraw a candidate (reason required).
9. **View Onboarding Notes**: Access additional onboarding notes provided by HWL or the facility.
10. **Onboarding Complete**: Indicates the candidate is fully onboarded and ready to begin the assignment.
