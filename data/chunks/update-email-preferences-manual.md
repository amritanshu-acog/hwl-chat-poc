## Context

This process explains how a user can manually update or customize their email preferences on the HWL platform, including selectively subscribing to job titles and specific email notifications.

## Response

To customize email preferences manually on the HWL platform, follow these steps:
1. From the HWL menu on the left under the Settings tab, select 'Email Preference'.
2. Select 'Job Titles Subscribed' to add job title selections.
   a. If you want to subscribe to all job titles, select the 'Default to Select All Job Titles' button from the top right. If you do this, skip to step 10.
3. On the subscriptions page, click 'Select Specific Job Titles'.
4. In the 'Job Title Selection' notice, click 'Add Job Group'.
5. From the 'Job Group' dropdown menu, select the appropriate option, then click 'Add'.
6. The 'Job Titles Subscribed' box will display a number indicating the total number of job title subscriptions added. Additionally, the added job groups will appear under the 'Select Specific Job Titles' button. Numbers next to each job group will indicate the title subscriptions per group.
7. Navigate to the 'Email Subscribed' tab to update and customize notification types.
   a. Items marked in green WILL be sent via email, while items marked in gray will NOT be sent.
8. From the list on the left, update your desired email preference notices.
