## Context

To get a candidate approved for a facility's requisition posting, they must be proposed through the system and meet all requisition matching requirements.

## Conditions

**For Nursing, Allied, or Non-Clinical Candidates**:
- Add time-off requests using the calendar on the proposal page.

**For AP/Physician Candidates**:
- If a schedule is provided, review and confirm shifts on the calendar.
- Decline or accept shifts as needed.

**Propose Specific Titles**:
- Indicate the appropriate matching job title in case of multiple title matches.

**Expenses & Allowances**:
- For AP/Physician, add expense allowance requests via the candidate's 'Notes' section.

## Response

To propose a candidate to a requisition:

1. Navigate to the Requisition page in the HWL menu.
2. Locate the desired requisition.
3. Under the Action/Status column, select 'Propose Candidate.'
4. Review the requisition details, including job title, start/end dates, and other filters.
5. Identify candidates from the staff pool to propose, ensuring their credentials and title match the requisition.
6. Click 'Propose' for the desired candidate.
7. For Nursing, Allied, or Non-Clinical candidates:
   - Add time-off requests using the available calendar, marking dates with an 'X.'
8. For AP/Physician candidates:
   - Review the schedule, confirm shifts, or decline any as appropriate.
9. Click 'Submit Availability' to finalize the shift schedule.
10. Adjust contract start/end dates only within the allowed range.
11. Upload or modify required proposal documents based on the facility's needs.
12. Answer any related prescreening questions or attestations if applicable.
13. Certify the proposal after completing all sections.
14. Finalize the process by clicking 'PROPOSE.'

Completion:
- Once proposed, the candidate will appear under the Candidate Status column of the Requisition tab for review by the facility.

## Escalation

Contact your HWL Account Manager if unsure of required proposal documents or having issues with candidate matching.
