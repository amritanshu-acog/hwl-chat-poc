## Context

Users looking to view all details of a requisition can access these details through a hyperlink under the Requisition column.

## Response

To view the full requisition details:

1. Locate the desired requisition on the Requisition tab.
2. Look under the 'Requisition' column for the blue-text hyperlink.
3. Click on the hyperlink to open a pop-up box displaying the full requisition details.
4. Use the scroll bar on the right of the pop-up window to view all entered details.
